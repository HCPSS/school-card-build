Weather Cancellation

All Howard County Public Schools Open 2 Hours Late

All Howard County public schools will open 2 hours late today, Thursday, February 9. All morning sessions of half-day Pre-K and RECC are canceled.

All morning child care programs, sponsored by the Columbia Association and the Department of Recreation & Parks in Howard County public schools, will be delayed 2 hours.

The Board of Education meeting planned for 4 p.m. today will go on as scheduled.

More information about emergency / weather closings, including weather reevaluations, can be found on our Emergency / Weather Closings page.
